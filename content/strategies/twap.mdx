**Updated as of `v0.41.0`**

An easy to configure automated strategy that expands the opportunities of professional trading beyond market-making.

This is for traders who want to sell/buy a big amount of capital on the markets without moving prices.

## Creating a TWAP Strategy

Enter `create`and input `twap` to start the strategy configuration settings.

### Adjust the configurations of the initial strategy with the following commands:

### `config trading_pair`

### `config target_asset_amount`

### `config order_step_size amount`

### `config order_delay_time`

### `config order_price`

### `config trade_side`
