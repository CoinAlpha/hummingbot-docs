**Updated as of `v0.41`**

An easy to configure automated strategy that expands the opportunities of professional trading beyond market-making.

This is for traders who want to sell/buy a big amount of capital on the markets without moving prices.

## Start a TWAP Strategy

Type and enter `create` then input `twap` to start the strategy configuration settings.

![](/img/twap-connect.gif)

Adjust the configuration settings by typing `config` and the strategy command in the input pane.
